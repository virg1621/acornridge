The Acorn Ridge Chronicles are about Adele Baker, a 60-something widow who is struck by lightning and can now see dead people. In "One More Victim," Adele must convince her best friend, Mary Lou, that her powers are real, all the while solving the murder of a young college girl before the killer strikes again.
